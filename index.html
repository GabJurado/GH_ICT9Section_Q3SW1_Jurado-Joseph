Wandavision
Synopsis
Newlywed couple Wanda Maximoff and Vision move into the town of Westview in a black-and-white 1950s setting. They attempt to blend in despite Vision being an android and Maximoff having telekinesis and reality-warping abilities.
Wanda Maximoff, a powerful witch who's in grief and decides to use her abilities to create her own reality where she can be happy. She loses sight of right and wrong under the blurry pair of lenses given by her grief and sorrow.
Vision, the android lover of Wanda whom dies from prior events. Him and Wanda grew close during the death of Wanda's brother in which he acted as the shoulder she could lean on.
